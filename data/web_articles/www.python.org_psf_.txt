The Python Software Foundation is an organization devoted to advancing open source technology related to the Python programming language.
We support the Python Community through...
Grants
In 2024, the PSF awarded $655,000 USD to 257 groups or individuals in 61 countries around the world.
Infrastructure
We support and maintainpython.org,The Python Package Index,Python Documentation,
          and many other services the Python Community relies on.
PyCon US
We produce and underwrite thePyCon US Conference,
          the largest annual gathering for the Python community.
         Support from sponsors, attendees, PyLadies, and CPython enabled us to award more than $384,000 USD in travel grants to 254 attendees for PyCon US 2025.
